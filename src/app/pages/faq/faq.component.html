How do I setup a productivity profile?

You can setup a productivity profile by clicking on the "Add Profile" button on the top right corner of the Profiles page. You can then add preset tasks to the profile by selecting them in the 'Preset Tasks Input' field.

How do I create preset tasks?

You can add preset tasks by clicking on the "Add Task" button on the top right corner of the Tasks page

How do I create timesheets?

You can add timesheets by clicking on the "+" icon in any day on the interactive calendar in the Timesheets Page, you can also click on 'Open' icon in the header of a calendar date

How do I see my metrics by date?

After creating a timesheet, you can see your metrics by each date on the Timesheets Page, with metrics for each date the metrics are read as follows:
{{ '{RESULT}' }} {{ '{SUCCESFUL_RESULT}' }} {{ '{FAILED_RESULT}' }}
Hovering also gives you a tooltip with more details

How do I see my all my metrics?

After creating some timesheets, you can see your metrics on the Metrics Page, with total and average metrics data
